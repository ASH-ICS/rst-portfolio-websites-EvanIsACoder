The Impact of Technology on Sports Betting

By 2028, the sports betting industry is expected to reach $114.4 billion by 2028, this represents an 86% increase from 2021. This is a massive jump for the sports betting industry as well as the sports industry in general, and it’s not surprising given how sports betting has always been around for a while. with many people betting on their favorite players and teams to make some quick cash or in some cases losing it all when their team or player decides to take a quick nap midgame. However, this is heavily due to the rise of online sports betting.

With how popular phones are, more and more people are able to connect to apps to do their betting. This is a lot more accessible and easy than visiting a physical bookkeeper to place bets. Being able to track your bets in real time and adjustment is an additional pro to online sports betting, not to mention how you can now place bets during the game itself due to how fast and accessible technology is. Technology has also allowed dedicated fans to research teams and players in order to help them with their betting.
